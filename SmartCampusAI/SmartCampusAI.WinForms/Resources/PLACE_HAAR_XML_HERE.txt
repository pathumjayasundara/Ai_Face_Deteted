Place these two files in this Resources/ folder:

1. haarcascade_frontalface_default.xml  (REQUIRED)
2. haarcascade_eye.xml                  (optional)

Download from OpenCV official GitHub:
https://github.com/opencv/opencv/raw/master/data/haarcascades/haarcascade_frontalface_default.xml
https://github.com/opencv/opencv/raw/master/data/haarcascades/haarcascade_eye.xml

OR run this PowerShell command:
  Invoke-WebRequest -Uri "https://github.com/opencv/opencv/raw/master/data/haarcascades/haarcascade_frontalface_default.xml" -OutFile "haarcascade_frontalface_default.xml"
  Invoke-WebRequest -Uri "https://github.com/opencv/opencv/raw/master/data/haarcascades/haarcascade_eye.xml" -OutFile "haarcascade_eye.xml"
